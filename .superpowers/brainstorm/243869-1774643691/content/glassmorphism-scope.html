What should the glassmorphism treatment cover?

Your current UI uses Mantine's AppShell with solid dark surfaces. Let's figure out where the glass effect should apply.

A

Cards & Panels Only

Apply glass effect to content cards (series detail, episode rows, settings panels). Keep the header and sidebar solid. Least disruptive, easiest to implement.

B

Header + Sidebar + Cards

Glass treatment on the navigation chrome (header bar, sidebar) plus content cards. The full "floating glass" feel. More impactful but touches more components.

C

Full Overhaul

Everything gets the treatment: header, sidebar, cards, modals, dropdowns, badges, buttons. Complete glassmorphism aesthetic across the entire app. Largest scope.
